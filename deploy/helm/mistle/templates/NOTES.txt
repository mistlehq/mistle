Mistle chart installed.

This chart is application packaging only. It expects:

- a Kubernetes cluster that already exists
- external infrastructure such as Postgres and ingress controller to be managed by the operator
- required Mistle configuration to be supplied through values-backed env vars and Kubernetes Secrets

- deploy/helm/mistle/README.md
- deploy/helm/mistle/values.yaml
- deploy/helm/mistle/values-example.yaml
